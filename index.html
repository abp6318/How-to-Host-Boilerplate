Cisco AnyConnect for RIT

Cisco AnyConnect is something you'll need to host and view your website on RIT's Solace server. You can find your computer specific download here: https://www.rit.edu/its/services/network-communication/vpn

Hosting Through RIT Servers

Solace (Login via ssh)

To log into Solace from a terminal, enter the following: ssh ritUsername@solace.ist.rit.edu. Don't forget to change the username to your own. Once run, enter your RIT password, and you're in! To view your Solace directory you do have to use Cisco AnyConnect.

Banjo (Login via ssh)

To log into Banjo from a terminal, enter the following: ssh ritUsername@banjo.rit.edu. Don't forget to change the username to your own. Once run, enter your RIT password, and you're in! To view your Banjo directory you do NOT have to use Cisco AnyConnect.

To logout of an ssh connection, press Crtl+C

What now?

Once logged in, you can cd into the ./www/ directory and create your website here. I recommend making a new directory and putting your website there.

For uploading any existing material, refer to this site: https://www.simplified.guide/ssh/copy-file. Or you could look at the commands I pasted below.

Function Command Line Input Example
Single file, Local to Remote scp myfile.txt remoteuser@remoteserver:/remote/folder/
Single file, Remote to Local scp remoteuser@remoteserver:/remote/folder/remotefile.txt localfile.txt
Multiple files, Local to Remote scp myfile.txt myfile2.txt remoteuser@remoteserver:/remote/folder/
All files, Local to Remote scp * remoteuser@remoteserver:/remote/folder/
All files and folders (recursively), Local to Remote scp -r * remoteuser@remoteserver:/remote/folder/

You'll probably want to use the last example in the table to import all of the files and directories into the server.

Viewing Your Website

Make sure you have Cisco AnyConnect installed and running to view your site on Solace.

Solace

Your link will look something like this, followed by the path to your directory: https://solace.ist.rit.edu/~ritUsername/

Banjo

Note: Your the Banjo server URL starts with people and NOT banjo. Your link will look something like this, followed by the path to your directory: https://solace.ist.rit.edu/~ritUsername/
